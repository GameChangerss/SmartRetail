Smart Retail with iBeacons

Smart Retail with iBeacons is all about smart devices and smart technologies-by connecting smartphone to in-store beacons to make smart shopping experience for customers.

Proximity Marketing

Proximity marketing is the mobile marketing to target customers within a particular geographic area or location.

Smart Retail

Smart Retail gives customer a greater ,faster and smarter experience while shopping by using smart technologies.

Q-Less payment

Customers can simply scan and pay for their shopping using their smartphones, without visiting the checkout.

Engaging Customer

* Finding customers footprints inside store helps retailers personalize the experience by placing products at the right space or send sales promotions and discounts.
* IBeacons deliver simple offer messages ensuring there is no hard-to-sales pitch for optimizing in-flow management.
* This feature is extremely useful because customers might switch off in-app push notifications as they consider this as an invasion of privacy

Effective Advertising and Marketing Tactics Tool

It can serve your advertising messages straight to the user as soon as the user’s device comes within a beacon’s proximity within your store and also serves to enhance customer experience.

1. Easy in-store navigation
2. Effective & Efficient
3. Strategic inventory management
4.Easy payment processing
5. Proactive & Productive

6.Provides Real Time data then & there

Cheap and Easy to Adopt

Beacon hardware is quite affordable and cheaper and can be used instantly,so even if you are unsure if beacon technology will be successful for your brand, it is worthwhile and cost-effective to at least test it out.

Boosts the Power of Personalization

Beacons can send personal shopping recommendations based on previous purchases,so the consumer can see and hold those recommendations in person.Moreover, if a store is particularly crowded with all salesman occupied, consumers can still have a personalized, assisted experience by way of the beacons.
